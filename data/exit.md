# Title: Quit Vim
# Category: Exit
# Tags: quit, exit, close
---
Use `:q` to quit, `:q!` to quit without saving, `:wq` or `:x` to write and quit, `ZZ` to save and exit, 'ZQ' to quit withut saving. 

```vim
:q   " quit
:q!  " quit without saving
:wq  " write and quit
ZZ   " save and exit
ZQ   " quit without saving
```
